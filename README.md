

## Data Sources and Project Ideas

- **Our World in Data**
  - **Link:** https://ourworldindata.org/
  - **Description:** Massive repository of global datasets about health, population, economics, and environment.
  - **Project Idea:** Compare life expectancy trends across income groups.
  <br><br>
- **NOAA Climate Data**
  - **Link:** https://www.ncei.noaa.gov/
  - **Description:** Weather, ocean, and climate datasets.
  - **Project Idea:** Analyze long-term temperature anomalies.
    <br><br>
- **US Census Data**
  - **Link:** https://data.census.gov/
  - **Description:** Demographic, economic, and geographic data.
  - **Project Idea:** Explore income inequality across states.
    <br><br>
- **NBA Stats API**
  - **Link:** https://www.nba.com/stats/
  - **Description:** Player stats, game outcomes, historical performance.
  - **Project Idea:** Predict MVP race outcomes using player efficiency metrics.
   <br><br>
- **L.A. Open Data Portal**
  - **Link:** https://data.lacity.org/
  - **Description:** Crime, traffic, environment, and city operations data.
  - **Project Idea:** Map traffic accident hotspots using geolocation.
  <br><br>
